# Assembly Manual

## Box Assembly

1. Confirm note label and tongue geometry before folding.
2. Fold long sides, then short sides, then closure tabs.
3. Clamp the box square with a sacrificial spacer inside.
4. TIG tack corners and closure tabs.
5. Inspect tongue root and remove internal burrs.
6. Tap the tongue with a soft mallet and record raw pitch.
7. Tune before installing in the rack.

## Rack Assembly

1. Lay out boxes chromatically C3 through B3.
2. Install rubber isolation strips on rack rails.
3. Place boxes with 12 mm gaps.
4. Tighten retainers lightly; over-clamping kills sustain.
5. Play a chromatic sweep and check adjacent-box cross-talk.

## Tuning Handling

Remove a box from the rack before filing or grinding. Keep sparks and filings
away from adjacent tuned tongues.
