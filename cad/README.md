# CAD Workspace

Generated CAD, STEP, and DXF files may be added after the first SolidWorks pass.
This blueprint release keeps CAD as instructions and tables only.

## First CAD Targets

- Master slit-box part with twelve configurations.
- Rack assembly with 12 mm box gaps.
- DXF layer template for `CUT`, `BENDS`, `ETCH`, `COUPON`.
- Three coupon DXFs for C3, F#3, and B3.

## Stop Gate

Do not cut all twelve boxes until three coupon/prototype notes prove the root
radius, kerf, heat effect, and tuning trend.
