# Design Brief

Authority: concept/design-planning blueprint only, not fabrication-ready.

## Intent

Build a sheet-metal slit drum bank that plays a full chromatic octave from C3
through B3. Each note lives in its own welded rectangular resonator box so the
bank can be tuned and repaired one note at a time.

## Lane Contract

- Twelve small rectangular steel boxes, 200 x 100 x 80 mm.
- One top-face U-shaped tongue per box for the validated pitch.
- Optional second tongue per box for a fifth-above color pitch.
- 1.5 mm cold-rolled steel.
- TIG-welded seams and laser/plasma-cut tongues.
- Validate pitch within +/-10 cents and sustain at least 3 seconds.

## Acoustic Strategy

Primary tuning uses the cantilever-beam approximation for a U-cut steel tongue:
frequency rises as tongue length shortens and thickness/stiffness increase. The
Wolfram starter estimates primary tongue lengths around 70-100 mm for C3-B3.

## Authority

The beam model is a first pass. Real tongue root stiffness, kerf radius, heat
effects, and box coupling must be measured by coupon and prototype boxes.
