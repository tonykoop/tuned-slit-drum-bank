# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished tuned slit drum bank presented as a real physical instrument: twelve small rectangular cold-rolled steel boxes arranged side by side in chromatic order like a compact marimba, each box roughly the size of a thick brick and set into a shallow wooden-and-steel rack that holds them at a gentle player-facing tilt. Each box top plate carries a single laser-cut U-shaped tongue, the tongues stepping slightly in length across the row to imply the low-to-high chromatic progression from left to right; a few boxes show a second, smaller secondary tongue beside the primary. The steel reads as brushed cold-rolled gray steel with crisp folded edges and clean TIG-welded side seams, the boxes seated on thin dark rubber isolation strips with low rails between them. A pair of soft round-headed mallets rests across the front edge. Clean neutral studio background, soft directional studio light raking across the tongue cuts to reveal their shadowed slots, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, realistic scale cues from the mallets and rack, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

The tuned slit drum bank staged on a maker's workbench in a metal shop, the twelve brushed-steel boxes in their tilted rack catching natural window light from one side, faint dust and warm wood tones in the background, a pair of soft mallets mid-play hovering just above one box tongue. Shallow depth of field keeping the tongue field sharp and the shop softly blurred behind, environmental storytelling of a quiet just-finished instrument awaiting its first tuning pass, concept visualization only, photorealistic, no text labels.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of a single steel box top plate showing the laser-cut U-shaped primary tongue and its small radiused root, the narrow cut slot reading as a clean dark line in the brushed cold-rolled steel surface, a faint secondary smaller tongue alongside. Raking low-angle light to emphasize the folded edge, the weld seam running away from the tongue root, and the fine grain of the brushed metal. High detail, tactile metal texture, no measurements, no callouts.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no impossible mechanisms or floating parts; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
