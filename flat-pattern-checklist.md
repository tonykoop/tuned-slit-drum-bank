# Flat Pattern Checklist

## DXF Layers

| Layer | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| CUT | Outer blank and tongue slot cuts |
| BENDS | Bend centerlines |
| ETCH | Note labels, grain direction, and witness marks |
| CONSTRUCTION | Non-cut references |
| COUPON | Tongue-root and kerf coupons |

## Box Blank Gate

- Units: mm.
- Material: 1.5 mm cold-rolled steel.
- Inside bend radius: 1.5 mm starter.
- K-factor: 0.44 starter.
- Tongue root radius: 2.5 mm minimum.
- Tongue root must remain at least 3 x thickness from a bend line.
- Closure tabs must not overlap tongue vibration zones.

## Coupon Gate

Cut and tune three coupons before the full set:

- C3 long tongue coupon.
- F#3 middle tongue coupon.
- B3 short tongue coupon.

Fail the gate if laser/plasma heat hardens roots enough to crack during tuning,
or if pitch lands more than 100 cents from the starter model before trimming.

## Paper Fold Gate

Print one box flat pattern and fold a paper/card version before cutting steel.
Check that weld seams remain reachable and do not trap the top face after bends.
