# Learn To Play

The bank is chromatic from C3 through B3.

## Starter Exercise

Play slowly with soft mallets:

`C3 C#3 D3 D#3 E3 F3 F#3 G3 G#3 A3 A#3 B3`

Then reverse the line. Listen for notes that die early after rack mounting; a
short sustain may be a mounting problem, not a tuning problem.
