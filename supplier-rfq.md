# Supplier RFQ

Request prototype materials and cutting only. Do not ask vendors to tune the
instrument.

## Steel Sheet

- Material: 1018 cold-rolled steel.
- Thickness: 1.5 mm.
- Quantity: enough for twelve folded boxes, rack rails, and coupons.

## Cutting Requirements

- Preserve DXF layers.
- No automatic corner sharpening at tongue roots.
- Tongue root radius: 2.5 mm minimum.
- Return coupon scrap with the order.
- Do not apply finishing or coating.

## Privacy

No public portfolio or social posting for the prototype blanks.
