# Validation Checklist

## Coupon Gate

- Cut C3, F#3, and B3 coupons.
- Measure raw pitch before grinding or adding mass.
- Confirm roots do not crack or buzz.
- Update CAD length table if trend is more than 30 cents from starter model.

## Box Gate

- Build C3, F#3, and B3 prototype boxes.
- Confirm each sustains at least 3 seconds off the rack.
- Confirm TIG welding did not warp the tongue roots.
- Only then release all twelve boxes.

## Full Bank Gate

- Tune every primary tongue within +/-10 cents.
- Mount boxes with isolation pads.
- Verify sustain remains at least 3 seconds on the rack.
- Strike each note and measure adjacent-box response.
- Play a chromatic sweep without awkward reach or mallet collision.
